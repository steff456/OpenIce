* is the Open-Source Integrated Clinical Environment.

* is an architecture for an inclusive clinical ecosystem for medical devices and clinical applications.

* provides a framework for the integration of healthcare devices and systems into the broader Medical Internet of Things.

* utilizes Data Distribution Service (DDS) standard for publisher subscriber based communication.

* is a grant funded project to prototype a standards-based plug and play medical device interface.
